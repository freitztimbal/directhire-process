>> Another file needed is the POLO endorsement letter for the exemption in the ban for direct hiring. Usually, PH government does not allow direct hiring but they can give exception if the overseas company have not hired more than 5 Filipinos, otherwise it is mandatory to employ PH recruitement agency service. This endorsement letter should be given by POLO once they successfully verify the documents.

Employee can now proceed with PH procedures after receiving scanned copy of all the POLO verified files from Step 3 including the POLO endorsement letter. After receiving original copies of all the POLO verified files from Step 3 including the POLO endorsement letter. Employee can now proceed with this step. This will be the last step. After OEC is issued , it is now clear to fly out of the country.
